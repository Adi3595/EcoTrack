# TRACKER RULES

# Transportation

* Petrol car = high emission
* EV = low emission
* Public transport reduces score

# Food

* Non-veg meals add higher emissions
* Vegan meals reduce emissions

# Electricity

* AC usage heavily impacts emissions
* Renewable energy reduces emissions

# Shopping

* Fast fashion increases emissions
* Sustainable products reduce score

# Waste

* Recycling lowers footprint
* Plastic waste increases footprint

# Carbon Score Formula

Carbon Score =
Sum(Activity × Emission Factor)

# AI Recommendation Rules

IF transport emission > threshold:
recommend public transport

IF electricity usage spikes:
recommend energy-saving appliances

IF food emission high:
recommend plant-based alternatives
